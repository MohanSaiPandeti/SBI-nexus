# SBI-nexus

AI-Powered Proactive Banking Relationship Platform

> Banking that understands, predicts, and engages.

---

## Overview

SBI NEXUS is an AI-powered digital engagement platform designed to transform traditional banking from reactive interactions into proactive financial assistance.

The platform continuously analyzes customer financial behavior and provides:

- Spending Insights
- Bill Predictions
- Personalized Product Recommendations
- Financial Health Score
- Fraud Awareness
- Multilingual AI Assistance
